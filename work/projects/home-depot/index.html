Home Depot Jersey City

SBLM has provided architectural services for numerous Home Depot stores throughout the Tri-State area.

Project Details

105,000 Square Feet
Jersey City, New Jersey
Retail & Commericial

The location presented design challenges including the store's proximity to the Eastbound entrance of the Holland Tunnel, high-volume deliveries from large carriers, and customer vehicle access for loading purchases.
The modern design produces a dramatic visual icon using Home Depot's trademark shade of orange on curved sculptural accents that make up the building's main facade.
This store was awarded First Place for Stores over 100,000 sq ft in Chain Store Age's Retail Store of the Year Exterior Design Competition.
