Project History
The two level 331,000 square foot shopping center is located at the intersection of the Hutchinson River Parkway and Lafayette Avenue in the Bronx.
Anchoring the center on the cellar level will be a Target and a TJ Maxx. The first level will contain smaller retail stores, as well as parking on both levels.
