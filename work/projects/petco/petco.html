Project History
SBLM completed this 700,000-square-foot warehouse for Petco Animal Supplies. The project features multi-pick towers, battery charging stations, sorting conveyor systems, and numerous loading docks and support spaces.
SBLM has worked closely with Petco on its national rollout program and serves as the "prototype" architect and exclusive designer for new stores in the program.
SBLM works closely with Petco executives, project managers, and in-house space planners to ensure that each store is efficient and attractive.
