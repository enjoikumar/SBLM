DSNY Staten Island Garage

The design, which is sited among a series of retired landfills that are undergoing a metamorphosis into parkland seeks to personify the future vision of the area. The architecture aspires to become part of the grassy knolls it both occupies and creates. Major tenants of the design principles are sustainability, equity, resiliency, and healthy living. It accomplishes sustainability most profoundly by reclaiming an industrial site as a habitat. Taking great strides to use turf-architecture to improve the local environment and performance of the building.



13.5 acres of site development
120,000 Square Feet of new construction
Fresh Kills, New York
