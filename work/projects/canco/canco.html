Project History
The renovation of the landmarked American Can Company warehouse into luxury condominiums totaled 400,000 square feet. SBLM replaced over 2,000 windows, in addition to reconditioning the façade, mending the deteriorated masonry, and introduced new roofing elements.
Key characteristics of the building were preserved in the transformation, with the retention of the landmark's Art Deco Façade, saw-toothed roof, grand-scaled windows, and soaring celing heights. SBLM's unique composition included the embedding of a mezzanine below the skylights on top floor units, forming vivid duplex apartments.
