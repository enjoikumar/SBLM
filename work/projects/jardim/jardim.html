Jardim
As the executive architect, SBLM was hired by Centaur properties to work alongside Brazilian Design Architect, Isay Weinfeld, on his first project in the United States. Located in the heart of Chelsea, these luxury condominiums are perfectly placed to overlook the Highline. SBLM designed two towers, one north with large, south facing flass and one south containg a small footprint. Thirty-six residences are distributed across the two towers. Each floor contains one apartment, ranging from 1 - 4 bedrooms and penthouses. Containing plenty of outdoor space, there is a big attention to detail surrounding the gardens on the base of each tower.
