Project History
SBLM designed the Olivia, a mixed-use, 35 story tower on West 34th Street in New York City that includes spaces for entertainment, retail stores, and residential units. The 100,000 square-foot- Loews flagship theatre,located on the first five dloors, features 14 state-of-the-art screens with stadium seating for 3,500 moviegoers and was one of the first megaplexes in the country.
Located above the theatre complex are 85,000 square-feet of retail and office space that sit below 300+ residential units. The unites include studios, one-bedroom, and two-bedroom apartments, which are framed with six-foot high window enclosures that grant impressive views of Manhattan and the Hudon River.
