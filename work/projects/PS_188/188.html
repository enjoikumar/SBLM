Project History
SBLM designed a 7,000-square-foot stand alone ground-up gymnasium building whch feaatures a regulation basketball court with bleachers and locker facilities for 378 students which will also allow for community use. All materials used are energy efficient and enviormentally sensitive, complying with the NYC Green Schools Guide for new construction.
SBLM was called upon the feasibility of adding a new gymnasium and determined that it could be accomodated by utilizing a portion of the playground and relocating the Early Childhood Play Area.
Design highlights include exposed architectural steel framing on the facades with glass block and stack bond brick in-fill allong with insulated metal panels.The glass block allows for natural day lighting within the gymnasium. Interior features include natural hardwood flooring and exposed structure.
