Project Details

102,000 Square Feet
Brooklyn, New York
Educational
SBLM designed a new sustainable school building to expand the existing PS 163K. This ground-up facility will respond to program requirements for a primary school, and a District 75(special needs) school accomodating a total of 665 students.
The six-story school will include a full gymnasium, auditorium and cafeteria located on the cellar level and a roof-top playground. Designed under the SCA's Green School Guidelines, the new 163K school acieved the LEED - Silver certification
