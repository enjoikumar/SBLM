Modern Fold Styles

Modern Fold Styles asked SBLM to expand and renovate their existing offices while showcasing several of their products. The challenge for SBLM was to create a dynamic office environment that displayed many products without creating an overall muddled and cluttered showroom-like space. SBLM maximized the ceiling height, and clerestory and other large openings were created to allow more natural light.
