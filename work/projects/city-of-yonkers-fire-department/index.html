City of Yonkers Fire Department

Located in the Getty Square Neighborhood of Yonkers, New York, the City of Yonkers Fire Station 1 will serve the various needs of the Fire Department while strengthening the city's commitment to public service. For this new ground-up project, SBLM proposed a unique form that maximizes efficient site circulation while opening opportunities for accessibility.
