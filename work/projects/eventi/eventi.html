Project History
The complex, mixed-use building includes extensive, high-powered vertical circulation, three levels of below grade parking, multiple restaurants throughout and retail boutiques at street level. The core of the project has 300 contemporary residences, and over 250 luxury hotel rooms.
Sblm auditied and coordinated the ermitted construction docments for the entire project, successfully navigating the complex issues that arose from the integration of disparate program elements and trades.
The building landscaping includes a public, inviting, block-through plaza featuring an art-glass wall and fully configurable JumboTron screen. The full project occupies roughly 500,000-square-feet, and was constructed at a cost of $600 million.
