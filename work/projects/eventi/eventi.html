Eventi / Beatrice Residences
The Eventi includes extensive, high-powered vertical circulation, three levels of below grade parking, multiple restaurants throughout, and retail restaurants throughout, and retail boutiques at street level. The focus of the project has 300 contemporary residences, and over 250 luxury hotel rooms.
SBLM audited and coordinated the permitted construction documents for the integrated project and successfully navigated the intricate issues that arose from the inegration of contrast program elements and trades.
The building landscape includes a public block-through plaza featuring an art-gass wall and fully configurable Jumbo Tron screen. The full project occupies roughly 500,000 square feet and was constructed at a cost of $600 million.
