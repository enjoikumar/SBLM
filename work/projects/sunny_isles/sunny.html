The school was designed using a tilt-up concrete construction, which recieved the Tilt-Up Concrete Associatation Achievement Award with Excellence in the Education Division.


210,000 Square Feet
Sunny Isles Beach, Florida
Educational
Located directly off of the intracoastal Waterway, this 210,000 square foot facility for kindergarten through 8th grade was the first public school to be built in the Sunny Isles Beach Community.
The designated site presented a considerable challenge. A facility this size would typically be built on a seven-eight acre site. SBLM needed to design this school on 2.1 acres.
