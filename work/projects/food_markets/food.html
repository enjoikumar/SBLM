Food Markets
SBLM has a long-standing relationship with a national food retail chain, providing architechtural services that achieve successful enviornmentally responsible new store projects for over 30 locations in the tri-state area, Connecticut, South Florida, and the United Kingdom.
One of the challenges for SBLM is to create a consistent program, which emobodies the company's core values, while addressing the multitude of tasks found in project types running from ground-up new multistory and mixed-use buildings to urban development and repurposed existing buildings. Many of these projects have achieved LEED Gold and Silver Certification.
To achieve the highest levels of innovation and quality for each store, SBLM's core team stay actively involved in the project from the early states of lease negotiation, feasibility studies, and programming through design, CD and construction administration phases, to the store's opening.
