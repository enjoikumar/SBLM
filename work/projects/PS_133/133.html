PS 133
To serve the expanding student population in the surrounding districts, SBLM was commisione to design a new facility to hous two district primary schools.
The building features a full size gymnasium, auditorium, cafeteria, along with art, music, and science rooms while meeting the NYCSCA's Green School Guidelines
The new facility responds to the surrounding low-rise residential neighborhood by locating the greatist density of development along the site's commercial street, and relocating the community garden as buffer between the residential neighbors and the school's main play yard.
