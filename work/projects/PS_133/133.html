To serve the expanding student population in the surrounding districts, SBLM was commisioned to design a new facility to house two district primary schools.

116,000 Square Feet
Brooklyn, New York
Education

The new facility responds to the surrounding low-rise residential neighborhood by locating the greatist density of development along the site's commercial street, and relocating the community garden as buffer between the residential neighbors and the school's main play yard.
The building features a full-size gymnasium, auditorium, cafeteria, along with art, music, and science rooms while meeting the NYSCA's Green School Guidelines
