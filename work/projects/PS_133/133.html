PS 133K

To serve the expanding student population in the surrounding districts, SBLM was commissioned to design a new facility to house two district primary schools.

116,000 Square Feet
Brooklyn, New York
Education

The new facility responds to the surrounding low-rise residential neighborhood by locating the greatest density of development along the site's commercial street and relocating the community garden as a buffer between the residential neighbors and the school's main play yard.
The building features a full-size gymnasium, auditorium, cafeteria, along with art, music, and science rooms while meeting the NYSCA's Green School Guidelines.
