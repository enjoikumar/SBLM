Project History
SBLM has provided architectural services for numerous Home Depot Stores throught the Tri-State Area.
The location presented design challenges including the store's proximity to the Eastbound entrance of the Holland Tunnel, high-volume deliveries from large carriers, and customer vehicle access for loading purchases.
The modern design produces a dramtic visual icon using Home Depot's trademark shade of orange on curved sculptural accents that make up the building's main façade.
