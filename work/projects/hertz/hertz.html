Project History
SBLM completed a major 78,000-square-foot site reconfiguration of the customer service center facility at the JFK International Airport. The project included a new 10,000-square-foot, two story customer service building, a new 60,000-square-foot raised parking deck, and the renocation and expansion of an existing 8,000-square-foot maintenance building.
SBLM"s expertise in contruction coordination with Hertz, various contract suppliers, adn the Port Authority of New York and New Jersey allowed dauly operation of the facility to continue uninterrupted throughout the construction process.
Due to the proximity to the AirTrain, the design of the flass roof and facade needed to deal with vibrations, wind loads, and water penetration.
SBLM's innovatove design included a transparent and contemporary structural glass wall and roof system that provides and accessible and open feeling.
