Kohl's NYC Design Office

SBLM administered interior design, architecture, and engineering services for its 40,000 sq ft office space. The three floors serve as Kohl's New York Design Studio; home to the design team for many of Kohl's signature brands. Kohl's requested a contemporary office that would cultivate the collaboration of ideas among the staff.
