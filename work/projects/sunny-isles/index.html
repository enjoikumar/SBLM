Sunny Isles Beach Community School

The school was designed using a tilt-up concrete construction, which received the Tilt-Up Concrete Association Achievement Award with Excellence in the Education Division.


Project Details

210,000 Square Feet
Sunny Isles Beach, Florida
Educational
Located directly off of the intracoastal Waterway, this 210,000 sq ft facility for kindergarten through 8th grade was the first public school to be built in the Sunny Isles Beach Community.
The designated site presented a considerable challenge. A facility this size would typically be built on a seven-eight acre site. SBLM needed to design this school on 2.1 acres.
